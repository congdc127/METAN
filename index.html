ABOUT
METAN EVOLUTION
Play now

Metan Evolution is a game application platform on Blockchain network. The game has NFT items with different and unique characteristics, features and rarity. Rewards in-game are organized according to GameFi architecture, allowing players to collect more NFT items and increase their values over time

Mechanism of Action
Free to play
Immerse in the fantasy world of METAN EVOLUTION Game - the exceptional Teamfight Tactics RPG with visually-stunning 2D graphics.
Play to earn
Lead your Metans into action-filled battles and claim rewards including Metan token and valuable NFT assets.
GameFi
Trade your NFTs on a full-stack Marketplace or utilize those assets in METAN ecosystem to maximize profits through DeFi features such as Staking, Lending & Borrowing, NFT Collateral.
Renting Metan
If the player does not have enough money to purchase a Metan, they can rent one from the system.
GAME PLAY
Players summon Metans and lead them into battle in monster-infested areas. At the same time, platers can take their Metans to compete against each other in Arena and win rewards.
